Summary

TIs new family of mmWave radar sensors offer low power, small form factor, and low cost SoC solutions suitable for personal and consumer electronics applications. The IWR6432 family of radar sensors can be used for developing applications such as:

  1. Human Machine Interface (HMI) by using hand and finger gestures in Smart Watches, Fitness equipment, Earbuds, Wireless Speakers, and more
  2. Vital Signs monitoring such as heart rate and breathing rate in TVs, Notebook PC, and Bedside monitors
  3. Presence detection, tracking, and classification for human vs non-human motion for personal and consumer electronics equipment. Onlooker presence detection for Notebook PC security applications
