Summary

Customers are using TIs mmWave radar sensors today to enable high accuracy level measurements of liquids and bulk materials in various industries including food and beverage, wastewater management and process industry. Our radar sensors offer non-contact, maintenance free measurement of fluid-levels up to 80m with extremely high distance accuracy of <1mm coupled with low power consumption targeting 4-20mA loop powered systems.

TIs Offering

What can TIs IWR1443 mmWave radar sensor offer for Level Sensing?

  1. <1mm distance accuracy enabling granular tank volume control
  2. Low energy consumption of <18mJ/measurement
  3. Measurement minimally influenced by temperature and pressure variations compared to ultrasonic sensors
  4. Immune to foam, vapors, powder, dust and condensates
  5. Can measure solid/powder levels
