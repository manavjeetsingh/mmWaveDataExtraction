Summary

With robots working in greater proximity to humans than before, they must have advanced sensing capabilities to recognize and avoid potential collisions to minimize risk of injury. Customers are using TIs industrial mmWave sensors to enable not only distance measurement but also relative velocities of people in the scene that can be used to trigger robot slow down or stop depending on proximity and speed of approach. TI mmWave radar brings additional value over vision and LiDAR based sensors by seeing in challenging environments such as rain, smoke, poor lighting and dusty factory conditions.

TIs Offering

What can TIs IWR6843/IWR6843AOP mmWave radar sensors offer for AGV/AMRs?

  1. 3D obstacle detection and tracking for path planning and autonomous navigation while enabling fast update rate (10s of fps) and wide azimuth area coverage up to 130 degrees
  2. IEC 61508 SIL 2 certified to enable replacement of expensive safety certified LiDAR sensors for safe human presence detection
  3. Increased angular resolution with imaging radar provides the ability to detect boundaries and navigate more accurately in busy environments
  4. Enable reliable detection of glass walls/doors over other sensing technologies like vision/LiDAR that sees through them
  5. Highly integrated offering including everything from radar front-end, antenna built-in MCU and simple SPI interface to report static and moving obstacles in the path of the AGV, all fitting in a 15x15 mm2 component
  6. A broad portfolio of 15+ options in 60GHz and 77GHz frequency bands offering feature scalability and ease of deployment in different regions to closely match your needs
