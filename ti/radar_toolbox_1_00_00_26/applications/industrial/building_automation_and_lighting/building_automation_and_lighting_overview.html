Summary

mmWave radar sensors offer a host of functionality that improve the effectiveness and efficiency of many building automation and lighting applications. The IWR family of radar sensors can be used to reliably and accurately sense the presence or absence of a subjects, count the number occupants in an area, set several areas of observation, determine precise location of persons/objects, determine the direction and speed of a subject and more.
