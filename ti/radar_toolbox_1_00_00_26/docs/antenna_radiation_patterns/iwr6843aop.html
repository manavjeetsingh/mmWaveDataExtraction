Introduction

IWR6843AOP EVM antenna is designed to be operated in 60GHz to 64GHz frequency range. The IWR6843 antenna-on-package (AoP) evaluation module (EVM) is an easy-to-use mmWave sensor EVM with integrated, short-range, wide field-of-view (FoV) AoP technology.

IWR6843AOP EVM Antenna Picture & Dimensions

The IWR6843AOPEVM includes four receivers and three transmitters. It operates at 4-Ghz bandwidth from 60 to 64 GHz, with a maximum output power of 12 dBm and a maximum antenna gain of about 5.2 dBi around 60 GHz.
